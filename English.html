English project topic- summary on Ila Sachani

Ila Sachani , an Indian Kathiawar embroidery artist overcame physical challenges in her life. She learned Kathiawar embroidery using her feet under the guidance of her mother and her grandmother. She has many skills. Ila was born in Amreli which is in Gujarat. she was born into a farmer's family. But her life was not easy. Because she was born without functioning hands. because of that her life was hard. But Ila's mother encouraged Ila and she was very focused to overcome her challenge. Ila's mother was skilled in the traditional art of Gujarat of Kathiawar embroidery which is famous in Gujarat . so she decided to share this with Ila. Her mother taught her to create many beautiful patterns using her feet. But it was a very hard task for Ila. But Ila accepted this challenge with confident and determination. so she started practicing with her feet to do other activities. she also learned Kachhi, Kashmiri, Lucknowi and many more too.

Ila's Kathiawar embroidery reached the government office in Surat. They were interested by Ila's story and were awestruck by her Kathiawar drawing. Ila became famous by her embroidery done by her feet. Ila Sachani also participated in national levels. she received many rewards and honors. with her hard work and will power and presentation she was the receiver of the president's medal. the most important change was the new independence. she proved to her changes that hard work and will power is the only thing to get success and victories in life.
