What is the main purpose of the GSAT satellite?

the GSAT satellite are india's indigenously developement communications satellites used for digital audio data and video.
broadcasting for both military and civilian users. As of november 2018, 19 GSAT satellite of ISRO (Indian satellite resarch organisation )
have been launched out of which 15 satellites are currently in service.

How GSAT satellites help in weather pridiction?

The geostationary satellite (GSAT),help improve weather predication by providing data about the Earth's atmospheres,ocean and land surfaces.

visible - light images

These images are easy to see and can show clouds, lakes, forests, mountains, snow, ice, fires and pollution.

Imaging during severe weather

GSAT can be commanded to take images every 5-15 minutes during severe weather outbreaks.

How GSAT satellites help with navigation?

GSAT satellites help with navigation through the satellite based augmentation system (SBAS) and the GPS aided Geo augmented navigation (GAGAN) payload.

GSAT satellites navigation recievers use signals from multiple satellites to reduce errors. they use techniques like Kalman filtering to combine the data into a single estimate for position,time etc.
